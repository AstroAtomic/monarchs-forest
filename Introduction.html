This campaign is officially designated as a **Horror-Class Scenario**. While core elements involve anomalies and institutional secrecy, interludes of light-hearted moments are expected.

ENGAGEMENT PROTOCOL: PLAYER BEHAVIOR DIRECTIVE

[INTERNAL NOTICE — CANDIDATE CONDUCT REGULATIONS]

This operation is classified as a **narrative-integrated deployment**. Field agents (hereafter referred to as "players") are expected to exercise **independent decision-making** within a world that functions with logic, consequences, and changing scenarios.

Upon entry, all agents will be **grounded in accessible knowledge** to make informed navigational, financial, and interpersonal choices. While the wider objective remains undisclosed, the deeper narrative will unravel progressively through investigation, dialogue, and active role engagement.

The mission does not adhere to linear or gamified structures. This is a **story-first operation**. The Worldmaster retains domain over world logic, faction behavior, and environmental progression. Each player, however, has total autonomy over their character’s beliefs, reactions, and actions.

Participation requires interaction and collaborative storytelling.

IMPERIAL DOSSIER: ASSIGNMENT 7A-44/BETA

[IMPERIAL DOSSIER 7A-44/BETA — CLASSIFIED - RANGER DEPLOYMENT: STARLING DISTRICT]

Following public circulation of a seemingly generic imperial notice regarding "Ranger Opportunities" in the Starling District, an abnormally high compensation range raised attention among independent operatives and mercenary groups. You — along with a few select applicants — passed through multiple layers of vetting, including formal interviews and non-disclosed background inquiries.

You have been **accepted** into the program.

Official travel orders have now been issued. You are en route to the **Capital City of Elanora**, where your presence has been requested at the **Royal Administration Building**. There, you will report to a man known only as **Sam** — an agent operating within the administrative core. The nature of your assignment has not been disclosed. All communications moving forward will occur on-site under sealed protocol.

Note: The unusually generous payment package implies the presence of covert operations, instability, or undisclosed threats in the target region. Caution is advised.

[END FILE]

ENTRY DOSSIER: CHARACTER QUALIFICATION FILE

[CANDIDATE REVIEW – CUSTOMIZATION AUTHORIZED]

When constructing a field operative, align background, skillset, and motive with campaign context. Why was this individual accepted into a high-paying imperial post? What compelled them to relocate? Consider occupation, history, and personal drive.

This is a **horror-class environment** — ultra-optimized, world-breaking builds are discouraged. Overpowered archetypes (e.g., “Paladin of a Thousand Suns”) may feel out of place.

**Homebrew content is permitted and, in some cases, recommended.** If using base classes like Wizard or Paladin, you may adapt or import nature-tied variations to reflect regional themes.

Non-human races (excluding humans, dwarves, and high elves) are considered rare in the Starling District. You are encouraged to generate custom lore and origin details for your race, homeland, or culture. The wider world outside Starling is deliberately undefined for your backstory.
