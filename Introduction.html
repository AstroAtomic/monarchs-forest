Introduction

What type of campaign is this?

This is an SCP-style horror campaign. The tone throughout will sometimes be serious and other times lighthearted.

Expectations

This is a detail-rich campaign where player agency is key. You’ll begin grounded in a world that offers just enough structure to make your own choices—where to go, what to buy, and how to act.
The deeper story will reveal itself gradually, as you explore and interact with the world around you. Roleplaying is central to this experience, and your decisions will shape the narrative in meaningful ways.
The goal of this campaign isn’t to mimic a video game. it’s to tell a living story. I’ll guide the world, but you, my friends, will shape it through the characters you bring to life.
