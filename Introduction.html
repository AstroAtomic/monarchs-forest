INTRODUCTION

What type of campaign is this?

This is an SCP-style horror campaign. The tone throughout will sometimes be serious and other times lighthearted.

Expectations

This is a detail-rich campaign. Players will start grounded with enough information to make decisions on their own on where they want to go, what they want to buy, and how to act. Players will slowly be able to gradually reveal the deeper plot. Role playing is a big part of this campaign. The goal of the campaign is not to be a video game, but a story. I will have control over the world, and you my friends will have control over your characters.
